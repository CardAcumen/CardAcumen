Card Acumen ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We do not sell your personal information — ever.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information collected automatically when you use our platform, and information from third-party sources where applicable.

Information you provide:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you create an account or join our waitlist.
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely through our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Communications — messages you send us via email or contact forms.
  • Preferences — teams, players, or content settings you configure within the platform.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages viewed, features used, time spent, and click patterns within the platform.
  • Device & browser data — browser type, operating system, IP address, and referring URL.
  • Cookies — small data files stored on your device to maintain sessions and remember preferences. See Section 4 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, improve, and personalize Card Acumen. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account and subscription
  • Process payments and send billing communications
  • Deliver prospect alerts, market digests, and platform notifications you've opted into
  • Personalize your content feed and dashboard based on your tracked prospects and preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve platform features and performance
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Send product updates, new feature announcements, and promotional emails (you may opt out at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We will not use your data for purposes materially different from those described here without giving you advance notice and, where required, obtaining your consent.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party vendors who help us operate the platform, including payment processing (Stripe), email delivery (e.g., Resend or SendGrid), analytics, and cloud infrastructure. These providers access your data only to perform services on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or valid governmental request, we may disclose information as necessary.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may transfer to the acquiring party, who will be bound to honor this policy or notify you of material changes.
  • With your consent — we may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used. The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function (authentication, session management). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand aggregate usage patterns so we can improve the product. We use privacy-friendly analytics tools and do not share this data with advertisers.
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and customizations between visits.

You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain platform features. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell behavioral data to advertisers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you cancel your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Email addresses collected via the waitlist will be retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of marketing — unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.
  • Withdraw consent — where we rely on your consent to process data, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@cardacumen.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and access controls that limit who can view your data internally.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at hello@cardacumen.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Card Acumen is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on the platform at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Your continued use of Card Acumen after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
